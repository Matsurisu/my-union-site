Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 24, 2025

At StaticSiteLab, we take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information on our static websites.

Since we operate primarily as a static website with minimal user interaction, our data collection is limited. However, we believe in transparency about any data that may be collected through third-party services.

Information We Collect

1. Automatically Collected Information

When you visit our website, certain information may be automatically collected by our hosting provider and any third-party services we use:

  • IP address
  • Browser type and version
  • Operating system
  • Referring website
  • Pages visited and time spent on pages
  • Date and time of access

2. Information You Provide

Currently, our static website does not include forms or user accounts. If we add interactive features in the future that collect personal information, we will update this policy accordingly.

Third-Party Services

Analytics Services

We may use third-party analytics services (such as Google Analytics) to understand how visitors interact with our website. These services collect data as described above.

Advertising Services

If we display advertisements (such as Google AdSense), these services may use cookies and similar technologies to serve relevant ads based on your browsing behavior.

External Links

Our website may contain links to external sites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of these external sites.

Cookies and Tracking Technologies

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device when you visit a website. They help websites remember your preferences and improve your browsing experience.

How We Use Cookies

As a static website, we use minimal cookies. However, third-party services we integrate (analytics, advertising) may use cookies. You can control cookie settings through your browser.

Managing Cookies

Most web browsers allow you to control cookies through their settings. You can usually find these settings in the "Options" or "Preferences" menu of your browser.

Data Protection and Security

Security Measures

We implement appropriate technical measures to protect any data we collect. However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure.

Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights regarding your personal data, including access, correction, and deletion rights.

Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we make changes, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page.

Note: We recommend checking this page periodically for any changes. Your continued use of our website after any modifications indicates your acceptance of the updated policy.
